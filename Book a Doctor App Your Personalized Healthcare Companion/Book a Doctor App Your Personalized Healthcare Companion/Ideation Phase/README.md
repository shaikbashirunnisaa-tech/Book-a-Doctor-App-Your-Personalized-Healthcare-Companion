# Ideation Phase

## Project Title
Book a Doctor App: Your Personalized Healthcare Companion

## Problem Statement
Many patients face difficulty in finding doctors, booking appointments, and managing their medical consultations. Traditional appointment booking methods are time-consuming and inefficient.

## Proposed Solution
Develop a MERN Stack web application that enables patients to register, search doctors, book appointments, and manage their healthcare online.

## Objectives
- Provide an easy doctor appointment booking system.
- Reduce waiting time.
- Improve communication between doctors and patients.
- Offer a secure and user-friendly platform.

## Expected Outcome
A complete online healthcare appointment booking system with separate modules for patients, doctors, and administrators.
